As Team Captain and Suspension Lead for our Formula Student team, I led the design and optimization of the vehicle's critical systems while managing a multidisciplinary team of engineers.

Key Achievements:

  • Performed CFD simulation of a parametrized intake manifold using Ansys Fluent, achieving measurable reduction in pressure loss

  • Created MATLAB code to calculate load transfer and distribution, enabling precise vehicle dynamics modeling
  • Reduced suspension sub-system weight by 4% through design optimization and material selection
  • Handled complete design, packaging, and clearance of components using SolidWorks and CATIA V5
  • Conducted Finite Element Analysis on powertrain components and evaluated various manufacturing methods

Technical Responsibilities:

  • Collaborated with aerodynamics and powertrain teams for system integration
