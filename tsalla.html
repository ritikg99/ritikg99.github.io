Bangalore

October 2022 – July 2024

At TSALLA Aerospace, a defense-focused startup incubated at IISc, I contributed to the aerodynamic development of a fixed-wing VTOL UAV and a quadcopter system. I handled tasks from early-stage conceptual design through to prototype fabrication and testing.

I developed and refined 3D CAD models using CATIA V5 and SolidWorks, particularly focusing on battery locking mechanisms, fuselage structure, and landing gear. FEA using Ansys Structural ensured the designs satisfied Factor of Safety requirements under lightweight constraints.

  • Performed complete CFD analysis of aircraft and propeller using transient sliding mesh techniques.
  • Led the wind tunnel test process—from DFM and fabrication to post-processing of the results.

I implemented mesh and grid independence studies to establish simulation benchmarks and contributed to the development of an in-house 6DoF aircraft dynamics model in MATLAB alongside one teammate. The fast-paced and multi-disciplinary nature of the work honed both my simulation and prototyping skills.
